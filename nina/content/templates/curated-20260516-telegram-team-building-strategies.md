Team building that actually works:

**Stop forcing fun.** Mandatory "bonding" backfires. Let teams opt in.

**Solve real problems together.** Cross-functional challenges > trust falls. Fix a process, build trust as a side effect.

**Mix the format.** Not everyone thrives in escape rooms. Rotate: strategic workshops, volunteer days, skill swaps, offsite working sessions.

**Include remote properly.** Hybrid isn't an afterthought. Use parallel activities, not "Zoom while others do something fun."

**Measure something.** Retention? Speed to collaborate? Absenteeism? Gut feel isn't strategy.

**Leadership participates as equals.** Not as judges, not as absentees. Same tasks, same stakes.

**Repeat.** One event changes nothing. Quarterly rhythm minimum.

**Debrief properly.** What did we learn about *how we work together*? Capture it. Apply it.

We design team building that transfers back to the job. Not just a nice day out.
