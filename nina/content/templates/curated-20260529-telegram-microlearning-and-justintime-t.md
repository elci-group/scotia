Microlearning & Just-in-Time Training: Stop wasting hours on training nobody remembers.

Here's the reality: Your people need answers *now*, not a 3-hour module they'll forget by Tuesday.

**Microlearning** = bite-sized, focused, under 10 minutes. One skill. One problem. One solution.

**Just-in-time** = delivered exactly when needed. Before that client call. During that software crisis. Not six months prior.

Why it works:
- Retention jumps 80% vs traditional training
- Completion rates soar (because 5 minutes beats 5 hours)
- Applied immediately = actually useful

When we build these for clients, we strip everything non-essential. No corporate fluff. No "learning journeys" that go nowhere.

Real example: Sales team struggling with objections? 90-second video. Specific reframe. Used five minutes before the next call. Results that week—not next quarter.

The catch? You need to know what your people actually struggle with. Not what your LMS library says they need. That's where most organisations fail—they dump content, not solve moments.

We design for friction points. The awkward conversation. The new compliance deadline. The system nobody trained anyone on.
